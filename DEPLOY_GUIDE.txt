FILES IN THIS PACKAGE
─────────────────────
  index.html       → Your app (rename gaa-stats.html to this)
  manifest.json    → Tells phones it's an installable app
  sw.js            → Service worker (enables offline use)
  icon.svg         → App icon — convert to PNG (see Step 2)
  icon-192.png     → App icon 192×192 (you create this)
  icon-512.png     → App icon 512×512 (you create this)


STEP 1 — CREATE YOUR ICON PNGS (2 minutes)
────────────────────────────────────────────
 Option A (easiest):
   1. Open icon.svg in any browser
   2. Take a screenshot, crop to square
   3. Resize to 192×192 and save as icon-192.png
   4. Resize to 512×512 and save as icon-512.png

 Option B (best quality):
   1. Go to https://realfavicongenerator.net
   2. Upload icon.svg
   3. Download the generated PNGs
   4. Rename them icon-192.png and icon-512.png

 Option C (use any square image):
   Just use any 512×512 PNG as your icon.
   Save two copies: icon-192.png and icon-512.png


STEP 2 — HOST ON NETLIFY (5 minutes, free forever)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 1. Go to https://netlify.com → Sign Up (free)
 2. On the dashboard, look for the drag-and-drop area:
    "Drag and drop your site folder here"
 3. Put ALL these files in ONE folder:
      index.html
      manifest.json
      sw.js
      icon-192.png
      icon-512.png
 4. Drag that entire folder onto Netlify
 5. Netlify gives you a URL like: gaa-stats-abc123.netlify.app
 6. (Optional) Click "Domain settings" to rename it to something
    like: gaa-stats.netlify.app

 ✅ Your app is now live and installable!


STEP 3 — INSTALL ON iPAD / iPHONE
────────────────────────────────────
 1. Open Safari on the iPad/iPhone
 2. Go to your Netlify URL
 3. Tap the Share button (box with arrow pointing up)
 4. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
 5. Tap "Add" to confirm
 6. The GAA Stats icon appears on your home screen!

 ⚠️  MUST use Safari on iPhone/iPad — Chrome won't show
     the "Add to Home Screen" option on iOS.


STEP 4 — INSTALL ON ANDROID TABLET / PHONE
────────────────────────────────────────────
 1. Open Chrome on the device
 2. Go to your Netlify URL
 3. Chrome will show a banner: "Add GAA Stats to Home Screen"
    — OR — tap the 3 dots menu → "Add to Home Screen"
 4. Tap Install
 5. The app icon appears on your home screen!


OFFLINE USE
────────────
 Once installed, the app works FULLY OFFLINE.
 Perfect for using at the pitch with no signal.
 All match data is stored on the device.


SHARING WITH YOUR CLUB
───────────────────────
 Just share the Netlify URL with anyone.
 They can use it in a browser OR install it as an app.
 No App Store. No download fee. No account needed.


UPDATING THE APP IN FUTURE
────────────────────────────
 If you update your index.html, just drag the new folder
 onto Netlify again — it deploys in seconds.
 Update the version in sw.js (change 'gaa-stats-v1' to
 'gaa-stats-v2') to force devices to get the new version.
